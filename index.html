Wall Clock

Focus Timer & Productivity Tracker

Wall Clock is a free, browser-based focus timer and productivity tracking application. Set timed focus sessions directly on a beautiful analog clock interface — start with a single click, set a goal, and track your progress in real time.

Sign in with Google or email to save your sessions, monitor daily streaks, tag your work by activity (Code, Study, Design, and more), schedule planned focus blocks, and view your focus patterns through an interactive heatmap spanning up to one year of data.

Wall Clock is free to use. No advertising, no data selling, no tracking. All session data belongs to you and can be exported at any time.
