Wall Clock

Focus Timer & Productivity Tracker

Wall Clock is a free, browser-based focus timer and productivity tracking web application. It helps users manage their time by setting timed focus sessions directly on a beautiful analog clock interface. Users can start a session with a single click, set a goal end-time, and track their progress in real time.

Registered users can log in with Google or email to save their focus sessions, view their complete session history, monitor daily and weekly streaks, and analyse their focus patterns through an interactive heatmap spanning up to one year of data. Sessions can be tagged by activity type (Code, Study, Design, Exercise, etc.) and exported at any time as JSON.

Wall Clock is free to use. No advertising, no data selling, no tracking. All session data belongs to the user. Built with React, TypeScript, and Supabase.
